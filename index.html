Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Localdealer collects, processes, stores, shares, and protects user data. This policy complies with GDPR and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible is Localdealer. We determine how and why personal data is processed.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name, mobile number, email
  • Authentication Data: OTP verification logs
  • Location Data: Approximate or GPS-based location
  • Lead Data: Property listings, buyer/seller preferences
  • Usage Data: Click behavior, session logs
  • Device Data: Device ID, OS, IP address

3. Purpose of Processing

  • To provide lead generation and property discovery services
  • To match buyers with sellers and dealers
  • To personalize content and recommendations
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized usage
  • To improve system performance and analytics

4. Legal Basis

  • User consent at signup/login
  • Contractual necessity to provide services
  • Legitimate interest (fraud detection, security)

5. Consent Management

Users provide consent during registration. Consent can be withdrawn by deleting the account or contacting support.

6. Lead Data & Access Control

  • Contact details (phone/email) are hidden by default
  • Dealers get limited access until user interaction or unlock
  • Phone numbers may be masked (e.g., 98XXXXXX21)
  • Lead access is logged and monitored for misuse

7. Dealer Restrictions

  • Strictly prohibited from reselling or redistributing leads
  • Bulk calling, scraping, or exporting leads is not allowed
  • Violations may lead to suspension, permanent ban, or legal action

8. Anti-Spam & Abuse

  • Users can report spam or harassment
  • We may restrict accounts based on suspicious activity
  • Automated monitoring systems detect abuse patterns

9. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (OTP, analytics, notifications)
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment gateways (if applicable)

10. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Data may be processed outside India. We ensure safeguards like encryption and contractual protections.

11. Data Retention

We retain data:

  • Active accounts: until account deletion
  • Inactive accounts: up to 24 months
  • Logs: up to 12 months for security

12. User Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent

13. Security
